I used Midjourney v6 to generate the ramen illustration because I wanted a warm, painterly image that felt both playful and polished. Midjourney’s painterly rendering and color control made it a strong fit for the visual tone I was aiming for: inviting, slightly stylized, and rich in texture. Choosing an AI model was a deliberate creative decision—I wanted an image that could serve as the project’s visual anchor while still feeling handcrafted.
My prompt was: “Colorful digital illustration of a steaming bowl of ramen with exaggerated toppings, soft painterly style, warm lighting, high detail.” The result closely matched my intent: the composition, color palette, and painterly brushwork aligned with the mood I envisioned. I made small edits—cropping and resizing—to better integrate the image into the canvas and to harmonize its scale with the other assets. Ultimately, the AI-generated ramen became the focal element of the piece, informing my typography and layout choices and helping to unify the project’s playful tone and timed motion.
